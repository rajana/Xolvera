# Xolvera — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-06

This Privacy Policy explains what data Xolvera ("the app", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

By creating an account or using Xolvera you agree to this Policy.

**Contact:** rajanamathew@gmail.com

---

## 1. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the game.

### 1.0 Guest play
You can use Xolvera **without creating an account**. Guests can browse topics and play full sessions. While playing as a guest:

- We do **not** collect your email, display name, or any other personal identifier.
- Scores, answers, and progress from guest sessions are **not saved** on our servers and cannot be retrieved later.
- The information described in Sections 1.1, 1.2, and 1.3 below is only collected when you create an account or sign in.
- Crash logs and diagnostic telemetry (Section 1.3) may still be generated by our servers if a guest's request triggers an error, since this is needed to keep the service running. These records are not linked to a personal identifier.

### 1.1 Information you provide (signed-in users)
- **Email address** — used as your account identifier and for sign-in.
- **Display name** — shown on your profile and on the leaderboard.

### 1.2 Information generated by gameplay (signed-in users)
- **Game sessions:** the topic you played, when you played, current difficulty, score, total questions, and correct answers.
- **Answer history:** each question shown to you, the answer you submitted, whether it was correct, and how long you took.
- **Progress per topic:** total attempted, total correct, accuracy, and last played time.

### 1.3 Information collected automatically
- **Authentication token** — a sign-in token issued after you log in, stored securely on your device using Apple Keychain (iOS) or Android Keystore (Android) via `expo-secure-store`.
- **Basic technical data** — standard request metadata (IP address, timestamps) processed by our hosting provider for security and reliability.
- **Crash logs and diagnostic telemetry** — when the Xolvera Game API encounters errors or performance issues, server-side stack traces, request timings, error messages, and standard request metadata (IP address, timestamps) are sent to **Microsoft Azure Application Insights** so we can detect and fix bugs. See Section 5 for details and retention.

### 1.4 What we do **not** collect
- We do **not** collect your real name, address, phone number, or payment information.
- We do **not** access your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, location, or health data.
- We do **not** use third-party advertising SDKs.
- We do **not** track you across other apps or websites.
- We do **not** use any analytics SDK inside the mobile app itself; diagnostic telemetry is collected only on our backend (see Section 5).

## 2. How we use your information

Your data is used only to:
- Create and authenticate your account.
- Deliver questions, score answers, and track your progress.
- Show your name and score on the leaderboard.
- Diagnose bugs and keep the service running.

We do **not** use your data for advertising or profiling.

---

## 3. How your information is shared

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your **display name** and **score** are visible to other users on the public leaderboard. Your **email address** and **answer history** are never shown to other users.

We share data only with:
- **Microsoft Azure** — our hosting provider, where the Xolvera Game API and database run (see Section 4 for details).
- **Apple** — when you download or update the app via the App Store.
- **Google** — when you download or update the app via Google Play.
- **Law enforcement** — only if required by valid legal process.

---

## 4. Data storage and security

- Account data, game sessions, and answer history are stored by the **Xolvera Game API**, hosted on Microsoft Azure App Service (region: **East US 2**) at `https://mathgames-prod.azurewebsites.net`.
- Sign-in tokens on your device are kept in Apple Keychain (iOS) or Android Keystore (Android) via `expo-secure-store`. Tokens never leave your device except as a Bearer header sent to the Xolvera Game API.
- All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS / TLS.

No system is perfectly secure, but we use reasonable measures to protect your data.

---

## 5. Third-party services

Xolvera relies on the following third-party services to operate. Data described below may be processed by these providers under their respective privacy policies.

### Microsoft Azure (App Service)
- **Provider:** Microsoft Corporation
- **Purpose:** Hosts the Xolvera Game API at `https://mathgames-prod.azurewebsites.net` (region: **East US 2**), which handles authentication, game sessions, scoring, leaderboards, and history.
- **Data processed:** Email address, display name, server-issued account identifier, authentication tokens, game answers, scores, session metadata, and standard request metadata (IP address, timestamps).
- **Privacy policy:** https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement

### Microsoft Azure Application Insights
- **Provider:** Microsoft Corporation
- **Purpose:** Collects crash logs and diagnostic / performance telemetry from the Xolvera Game API so we can detect and fix bugs and monitor service health.
- **Data processed:** Server-side stack traces, exception details, request and dependency timings, error messages, performance counters, IP addresses, and standard request metadata. We do not deliberately attach personal identifiers to telemetry; however, when an error occurs while servicing your request, the telemetry record may include the API path you called.
- **Retention:** Up to 90 days (Application Insights default), after which records are automatically purged.
- **Privacy policy:** https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement

### Apple Keychain / Android Keystore (via `expo-secure-store`)
- **Purpose:** Securely stores your authentication token on your device.
- **Data processed:** Authentication token only. Tokens never leave your device except as a Bearer header sent to the Xolvera Game API.

We do **not** use third-party advertising networks, in-app analytics SDKs, in-app crash-reporting SDKs, or cross-app tracking services. The telemetry described above is collected only on our backend, not from the mobile app itself.

## 6. Data retention

**Guest play.** No account data is created when you play as a guest, so there is nothing to retain — your scores and answers from a guest session disappear as soon as the session ends.

**Signed-in accounts.** We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from inside the app — open the **Profile** tab and tap **Delete Account** at the top of the screen. Once confirmed, we immediately and permanently remove your profile, scores, progress, and answer history. The deletion is irreversible.

If you cannot sign in to use the in-app option, email **rajanamathew@gmail.com** from your registered address with the subject **"Delete my account"** and we will remove your data within 7 days.
