# Xolvera — Account & Data Deletion

**App:** Xolvera
**Developer:** Novaria
**Last updated:** 2026-05-06

This page explains how to permanently delete your **Xolvera** account and all associated data.

---

## Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

The fastest way is in-app:

1. Open **Xolvera** on your iPhone, iPad, or Android device.
2. Sign in with your registered email.
3. Tap the **Profile** tab at the bottom of the screen.
4. Tap **Delete Account** at the top-left of the Profile header.
5. Tap **Continue** on the first warning.
6. Tap **Yes** on the final confirmation.

Your account is deleted **immediately and permanently**. You will be signed out and returned to the sign-in screen. The deletion cannot be undone.

---

## Option 2 — Request deletion by email

## What is deleted

When you delete your Xolvera account, the following is permanently removed from our servers:

- Your account profile (email address, display name, account ID)
- All game session data (topic, difficulty, scores, timing)
- Your full answer history (questions shown, answers you submitted, correctness)
- Your per-topic progress (totals, accuracy, last-played timestamps)
- Your leaderboard entries (daily, weekly, all-time)
- Your authentication token

The deletion happens immediately on our database. Standard backup snapshots (held for disaster recovery only) are overwritten on a rolling **30-day cycle**, after which no copies remain anywhere.

---

## What is kept

We do not retain any personally identifiable data after deletion. The only items that may persist are:

- **Anonymous, aggregated statistics** that cannot be linked back to you (e.g. "X users played Topic Y this week"). These contain no email, name, or user ID.
- **Server access logs** (IP addresses and timestamps of HTTP requests) kept by our hosting provider, **Microsoft Azure**, for **up to 90 days** for security and abuse prevention. These are not joined with your account data after deletion.
